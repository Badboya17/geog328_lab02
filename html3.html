Basic Info

Actor, Singer, Heartthrob, Action Star

Eddie Peng, whose real name is Peng Yu-yan, is a Taiwanese-Canadian actor and singer. Born on March 24, 1982, in Taipei, Taiwan, Eddie moved to Canada with his family at a young age. He began his career in entertainment as a host on a Taiwanese variety show, but he quickly transitioned to acting and eventually singing, establishing himself as a prominent figure in the Chinese entertainment industry.
